In \(\Sym_{4}\text{:}\)

\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{ccc} \Id \amp 1 \amp 1\\ (1\;2) \amp \frac{4\cdot3}{2}=6 \amp 2\\ (1\;2\;3) \amp \frac{4\cdot3\cdot2}{3}=8 \amp 3\\ (1\;2\;3\;4) \amp \frac{4\cdot3\cdot2\cdot1}{4}=6 \amp 4\\ (1\;2)(3\;4) \amp \frac{4\cdot3\cdot2\cdot1}{2\cdot2\cdot2!}=3 \amp 2\\ \end{array} \end{equation*}

Sommando i numeri nella seconda colonna, otteniamo \(1+6+8+6+3=24\text{,}\) come previsto.
